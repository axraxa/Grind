Hanamichi Sakuragi

  I'm tensai Sakuragi Hanamichi.I'm going to play basketball, because I'm the best basketball player.

  Throughout the manga, he is very short-tempered, boisterous, arrogant, aggressive, rude, and immature. He begins regarding himself as a "genius" even though he has not learned any skills.

  He also prided himself as the "Rebound King" when his rebounding ability had just started to surface, which was accepted and considered as valid.
