How to Build Your PC

Motherboard

  Moterboard(MoBo) is a thing where we put every pc part(CPU, GPU , RAMs , HDD , e.t.c.) and they connect (to each other) through MoBo . Motherboards have generations which are DDR1 , DDR2 , DDR3 , DDR4 and DDR5 ,from which DDR1 and DDR2 are outdated(very old to use), DDR3 is old too but still usable for low end pcs , DDR4 is new and DDR5 is newest generation (which is logically the best option).

CPU

  CPU is part of the pc which processes everyhing that's going on our computer.If we want our pc to render (images or videos or even games) fast we need to have strong (not outdated) CPU . There is two big companies which manufactures them (Intel and Amd) , nowadays market is so big that they are always,almost even (performance wise), but in the old days amd was falling back.So u can choose whatever company's CPU u want to buy(bias AMD better).

GPU

  GPU is PC part which we can say paints everything on desktop, for example if you are playing online-competetive game called CSGO and throw a smoke , that smoke pixels are drawn by GPU , CPU does it too but GPU is better at painting , making things beautiful more than CPU would.In GPU market there is also 2 big companies but AMD and Nvidia ,Intel manifactures GPUs too but they are not as high level as amd's and nvidia's. When you are choosing GPU you must consider Bottleneck to get best performance from your built PC.

RAM

  RAM (random-access memory) is essentially short term memory where data is stored as the processor needs it. To explain it easily , ram helps you to open 20tabs on google chrome , if you have not much RAM GBs to open 20tabs of google your PC will start laging,it will slow down and you will not be able to experience (for example) watching youtube at best performance your pc can do.

PSU

  PSU(power supply unit) connects on motherboard as every part of the PC , we connect our computer to electricity by PSU and everything connected on MoBo gets energy from it.So we can say that every PC part needs each other , because without psu they won't be able to power on , without ram and cpu you won't be able to run computer , as without hard drive or ssd because you can't run computer without operating system (OS) and it must be on hdd or ssd.

SSDs and HDDs

  HDD(Hard-drive-disk) and SSD(solid-state-drive) is just memory which contains our pc data and operating system . HDDs are slow but they are cheap and we can buy 1TB HDD for 120gb SSD for example. SSDs are more expensive than HDDs but they are way faster and it loads everything going on computer very fast. There are 2 types of SSDs on market which are Nvme and Sata , Nvme SSD gets placed on motherboard and is faster than sata SSD,on the other hand we can connect Sata SSD to motherboard by cable(still sata SSD is faster than any HDD).
